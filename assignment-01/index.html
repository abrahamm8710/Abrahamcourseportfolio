My Top 5 Video Games

These are my five video games that I like to play the most.

  1. Rocket League

    a free to play vehicular soccer game where players use rocket powered cars to hit a giant ball into the opposite team's goal.

  2. Fortnite

    A online video game created by Epic Games with several game modes, most importantly a 100 player battle royale where 100 players fight to be the last one.

  3. Horizon Forbidden West

    A single player game developed by Guerrilla Games.A sequel to Horizon Zero Dawn were the protagoinst Aloy explores the post-apocalyptic Amercian West to fight dangerous machines and explore the past in the 30th century.

  4. Sonic Racing:Crossworlds

    a fast paced multiplayer racing game where you race other opponents using Sonic Characters and Guest Characters within the Sonic Universe. It features a Crossworld mechanic where the leader of the first lap chooses a new track for the second lap, adding unpredictability to races.

  5. Fall Guys

    a free to play cross platform battle royale where 40-60 players compete against each other in a series of chaotic obstacle courses until there a single winner left. Players control a bean shaped character and must go through challenges to be the last one standing, winning the crown at the end.
